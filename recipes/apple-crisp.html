Apple Crisp Recipe

This apple crisp showcases apples at their best!
The combination of warm, tender apples and the crunchy
oat topping is one of my favorite desserts.
Delicious with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Apples: You'll need 10 cups of peeled, cored, and sliced all-purpose apples.
    • Sugars: This apple crisp recipe calls for white sugar in the filling and brown sugar in the topping. Flour: All-purpose flour thickens the filling and gives the topping some structure. Cinnamon: A teaspoon of ground cinnamon lends warmth that perfectly complements the apples' flavor. Oats: Use quick-cooking oats for the topping.
